Taimoor Tariq

I am a PhD student at the University of Lugano, Switzerland, advised by Professor Piotr Didyk. I am working on the ERC Starting Grant supported PERDY project, focused on display-specific perceptual optimization of graphics content to match the requirements of human perception. I received my M.S. degree from KAIST, working with the Image and Video Computing Group. I am currently also working with the Applied Perception Science team at Facebook Reality Labs, on efficient computational display algorithms for real-time VR-HMDs.

Research

I am broadly interested in various aspects of real-time computer graphics & human perception; especially the mapping of sensory data from the eyes into our low-level interpretation of visual reality i.e the perceptual basis for realism. My current work is directed toward perceptually optimizing the real-time graphics pipeline for future realistic VR/XR systems. Other areas I am interested in include Computational Photography, Gaze Analysis, and Psychoacoustics.
