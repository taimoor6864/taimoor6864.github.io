Cihang Xie

I am a 4th-year PhD student in Computer Science at Johns Hopkins University, advised by Bloomberg Distinguished Professor Alan Yuille. I received my M.S. degree from UCLA, and B.S. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

I have worked as a research intern with Kaiming He and Laurens van der Maaten at the Facebook AI Research (FAIR); Quoc Le at the Google Brain. I receive the 2020 Facebook Fellowship.
