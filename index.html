Research

I am broadly interested in various aspects of cognitive psychology; specifically, how does our visual system map physical reality into our percieved cognitive reality? My current work is focued on understanding how the human visual system compresses/processes visual information from the physical world; and using this understanding to perceptually optimize the graphics pipeline for future VR-HMDs.
